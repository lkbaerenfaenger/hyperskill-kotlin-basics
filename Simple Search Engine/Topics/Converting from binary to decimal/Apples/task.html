Apples

We are at the binary post office again.

The mail carrier decided to show you all the boxes that they've got at the moment. The first one can contain only one apple, the second – 2 apples, the third one – 4 apples, and so on.

How many apples can fit in the 8-th box?

Remember that when thinking about powers of 2, the first one is \(2^0\)!
