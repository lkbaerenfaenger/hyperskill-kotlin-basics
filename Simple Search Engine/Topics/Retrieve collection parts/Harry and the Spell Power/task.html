Harry and the Spell Power

Harry Potter is learning different spells. He has a list of spells and a list of power values. He must retrieve the first spells with the power greater than 50. Please help Harry print the Spell List.


Sample Input:
Expetum-98 Patronus-83 Axio-29 Lithigum-23

Sample Output:
[Spell(name=Expetum, power=98), Spell(name=Patronus, power=83)]



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
