Life is like a box of chocolates

Forest loves candy and math very much. Therefore, he decided to eat sweets according to a certain formula \((i + j) \bmod k = 1\), where \(i\) is the row number and \(j\) is the column number. Which candies will remain in a square box of size \(n \times n\) when he eats all the candies according to the formula?

function reggae(n, k):
    for i in [1, n]:
        for j in [1, n]:
            if (i + j) mod k == 1 then
                print("*")
            else:
                print("o")
        println()                 //new line
